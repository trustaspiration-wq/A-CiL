Legal Status

Aspiration Cyber Intelligence Lab (A-CiL) operates as a research and innovation unit under Aspiration Trust, a registered non-profit entity in India.

The lab conducts independent research, academic collaboration, advisory, and capacity-building activities in cyber intelligence, AI systems, blockchain, and national-scale digital infrastructures.
